Frequently Asked Questions

What types of videos do you clip?
We work with any long-form content: YouTube videos, podcasts, webinars, tutorials, vlogs, gaming streams‚Äîanything over 5 minutes that can be broken into engaging clips.
How many clips will I get from one video?
It depends on your plan and video length. Starter gets 1 clip/day, Growth gets 1-2 clips/day, and Authority gets 2-3 clips/day. We focus on quality moments that drive engagement.
Do I need to send you raw footage?
No! Just send us the YouTube link or upload your video to Google Drive/Dropbox. We handle everything from there.
Can you post the clips for me?
Yes! All plans include posting. Growth plan posts to your page or up to 3 of our pages. Authority plan posts to all 5 of our pages plus your own channels.
What if I don't like a clip?
Growth plan includes 2 revisions per month. Authority plan includes unlimited revisions. We'll re-edit until you're satisfied.
How long does it take?
Clippings start 2-3 business days after purchase. Authority plan gets priority turnaround for faster delivery.
